# align-by-abode-cybersecurity-grc-framework
A practical security and compliance framework for a rental marketplace. It includes a Master Risk Register, a corporate Policy Manual, and a step-by-step Emergency Response Plan built to comply with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023 and ISO 27001 standards.
### Vendor Compliance and Acceptance/Onboarding Framework
An operational policy and third-party risk vetting pipeline for onboarding marketplace merchants.
* **Core Focus:** Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) legal identity mapping, Anti-Money Laundering (AML/CFT) screening, and user safety enforcement.
* **Key Deliverables:** Multi-tier compliance vetting pipelines (Stream A for individuals, Stream B for corporations) and a mandatory verification questionnaire.
* **Regulatory Focus:** CAMA 2020, Money Laundering Act 2022, and Section 24 of the NDPA 2023.
